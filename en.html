Our Mission

Creating initiatives and solutions through citizen collaboration.

Nanay was a web platform that connected people in need with volunteers willing to help. A bridge of solidarity born in Ecuador during the COVID-19 pandemic.

April 2020 - December 2022

Meaning

Nanay means pain in Quichua and mother in Filipino

Like a mother seeking to soothe pain, our team aimed to give voice to social groups affected by the pandemic.

Our Drive

Your well-being drives us

We were born as a contribution to the community, to create a more supportive, inclusive, and sustainable environment.

Presence in 7 countries

We offered and received help from anywhere in the world

We had requests in Bolivia, Ecuador, Chile, Colombia, Guatemala, Mexico, and Panama.

Our Story

The journey of Nanay

April 2020
Nanay is born
In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, Nanay is born in Ecuador as a web application to connect vulnerable people with volunteers who can offer help.
2020
Growth and community
The platform grows with help categories: food, hygiene supplies, provisions, and work. Over 900 people connect in the community.
October - December 2020
#25FamiliasNanay
With the support of volunteers and allies like Yoguibeard and Idonea, campaigns benefit 56 families in Ecuador with food and hygiene kits.
2020 - 2022
Presence in 7 countries
Nanay expands its reach to Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Guatemala, Mexico, and Panama, with help requests from different parts of the world.
2022
Model evolution
As pandemic conditions changed, Nanay evolved its vision towards entrepreneurship and dignified work opportunities as a driver of lasting change in society.
December 12, 2022
Closing of new requests
New help requests were closed, while maintaining existing ones with the hope that new volunteers could continue offering support.

Thank you for trusting Nanay

Two years later, pandemic conditions changed and we needed to evolve, changing our service model to adapt to this new reality.

We believe that entrepreneurship and the opportunity to have dignified work is vital to creating lasting change in society and improving people's quality of life.

Our commitment to helping and improving our users' quality of life remained as strong as from the first day of the pandemic. We thank you for your trust. We are certain that the path traveled left a positive mark on the lives of many people in our community.
